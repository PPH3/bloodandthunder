This is a short list of what I condider to be the best works of weird fiction. Reading Lovecraft's essay Supernatural Horror in Literature led me to track down and explore some of the obscure works I had not previouly read.

Story Titles

  1. Melmoth the Wanderer
  2. Great God Pan
  4. The Willows
  5. The Yellow Sign
  6. The Monk

Story Selection

Lovecraft was something of a horror scholar. For his essay, he conducted research that involved studying weird fiction from all across the world beginning from ancient times. The survey includes stories from many countries and he opines as tho which are the strongest. The titles listed above represent my own preferneces.
